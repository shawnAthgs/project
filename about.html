About CyberHelp

CyberHelp is a website created to educate people about common online scams and how to avoid them. With the rise of the digital world, scams have become more advanced and harder to detect.

Our goal is to provide simple, clear examples of scams such as phishing, fake checks, romance scams, and ransomware. By understanding how these scams work, people can better protect themselves.

About Me

Hi! My name is Shawn. I created this website as a project to help raise awareness about cybersecurity and online safety.

I’m interested in technology and want to help people stay safe while using the internet. This project is designed to make learning about scams easy and accessible for everyone.
