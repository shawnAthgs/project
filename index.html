Phishing Scam

Fake Check Scam

1. Feel the edges – fake checks may have rough edges.

2. Verify bank details carefully.

3. Watch for spelling mistakes.

Emergency Scam

Scammer pretends to be a family member in danger.

  • Urgent money request
  • Vague details
  • Unknown sender

Romance Scam

  • Fast emotional attachment
  • Love bombing
  • Fake personal stories

Ransomware Scam

Files locked and ransom demanded.

  • Bitcoin payment demand
  • Threats to delete files
  • Fake encryption message

Social Media Scam

Hackers steal social media accounts.

  • Fake login pages
  • “Is this you?” links
  • Password theft

Gaming Scam

Fake free items in games.

  • Fake Robux/V-Bucks
  • Trading scams
  • Fake verification sites

Verification Code Scam

Scammers steal login codes.

  • “Send me the code” trick
  • Fake friend accounts
  • Account takeover
